Smart Agri-Horticulture

Duration: May 2019

Github
Velanyantra webapp
Description:
  • Most agricultural food production in the modern day is performed in large-scale, mono-crop farms on huge plots of land. While it has been streamlined to produce huge amounts of food at a relatively cheap price, mono-crop farming puts a significant strain on the soil and the surrounding environment by using up specific nutrients for different crops, as well as using tremendous amounts of water.
  • Good water management using the well-designed system is critical for sustaining production and quality of produce, more specifically for horticultural crops.
  • Scheduling based on plant water balance in consonance with soil and climate is appropriate. The water has to be applied to the root zone to save the losses. Since a lot of losses are caused during the conveyance, it is essential that an appropriate delivery system is used which enhance the efficiency.
  • With the help of this gantry structure, we can overcome all the problems of when, where, and how water should be applied and also from the drawbacks of drip irrigation.
  • And also this system enables us to access each plant individually for analyzing its current progress and also pest and disease in plants can be identified and reported to the user with the help of image recognition and IoT.
  • The climate inside the greenhouse will be measured and maintained by an automated system with the help of various sensors and actuators. All the components in the remote location will be made easily accessible to users with the help of IoT technologies.
